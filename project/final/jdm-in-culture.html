The Rise of JDM Car Culture

Introduction to JDM Car Culture

JDM cars have become iconic symbols of automotive culture worldwide. Their impact can be seen in the tuning scene, street racing, and the growing car enthusiast communities that celebrate these vehicles.

JDM and Street Racing

JDM cars played a significant role in the street racing culture, especially in the 1990s. The Nissan Skyline GT-R, Toyota Supra, and other high-performance cars were key players in the underground racing scene.

Tuning and Modification
